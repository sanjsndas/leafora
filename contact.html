Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some of the common questions we receive. For more assistance, please reach out using the contact form.

What areas do you serve?

Leafora Garden Designs primarily serves clients in the New York metropolitan area, including the surrounding states. For certain projects, we also offer remote consultation and design services.

How long does a typical project take?

Project durations vary widely depending on the scope and scale. A small garden redesign may take a few weeks, whereas full-scale landscaping and forestry projects can take several months.

Can you work with sustainable and native plants?

Absolutely! Sustainability is key to our design philosophy. We prioritize native plants and eco-friendly landscaping techniques.
