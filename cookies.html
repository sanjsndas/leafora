Cookies Policy

Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how Leafora Garden Designs uses cookies and similar technologies on our website to recognize you, enhance your experience, and analyze website usage. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences, login status, and usage patterns.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality such as navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data on how visitors use the site to improve performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language or region.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads based on your interests.
  • Analytical Cookies: Gather information about website traffic and user behavior to help optimize our services.
  • Social Media Cookies: Enable sharing and interaction with social networks directly from our site.
  • Security Cookies: Help detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized access.
  • Preference Cookies: Keep track of your preferences and settings for personalized experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to personalize your user experience, provide analytics to improve our services, and show targeted advertising where appropriate. Essential cookies enable core functionalities and cannot be disabled without impacting website performance.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. You may choose to block or delete cookies, but this may affect website functionality. Additionally, you can update your preferences at any time via our cookie consent banner.

Your Choices

You have the option to accept or decline cookies using the cookie consent dialog at the bottom of the page. You may also change or withdraw consent at any time by clearing your cookies or adjusting your browser settings.

Cookies We Use in Detail

Our cookies include both first-party cookies set by Leafora Garden Designs and third-party cookies from analytics and advertising providers. For example, Google Analytics is used for site usage insights.

Third-Party Cookies

We use reputable third-party services such as Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and others, which may place cookies on your device for analytics, remarketing, and targeted ads. Please review their privacy policies for details.

Data Protection

Cookies do not store personal information directly, but some cookies track your behavior and preferences in an aggregated and anonymous form to help improve our website and services securely and responsibly.

Cookie Duration

Cookies may be session cookies, which expire when you close your browser, or persistent cookies, which remain until expired or deleted. Duration varies depending on cookie type and provider.

Legal Compliance

Our cookie practices comply with regulations such as the EU ePrivacy Directive, GDPR, and other applicable laws, ensuring transparency, user control, and data protection.

Cookie Preferences Management

You can manage your cookie preferences using the consent banner or through browser settings at any time. Disabling non-essential cookies may limit certain features and site functionality.

Security and Cookies

While cookies themselves do not typically contain sensitive information, we implement encryption and secure protocols (HTTPS) to protect your data during transmission and enhance overall security.

Cookie Policy and Privacy Policy Relationship

This Cookies Policy complements our Privacy Policy, which covers broader data collection, usage, and protection practices.

You can modify your cookie settings through the cookie consent banner or adjust browser preferences to control cookie behavior, including deleting existing cookies.

Cookies are generally safe and are widely used to improve website experience. We ensure all cookies used on our site are secure and comply with regulations.
