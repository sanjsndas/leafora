Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Leafora Garden Designs, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website privacy.html and engage with our services.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use our website or communicate with us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide when requesting services or contacting support.

We also automatically collect certain information about your device, browsing actions, and patterns, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs to improve user experience and security.

This information helps us tailor our services, detect and prevent fraud, and improve the website continuously.

Use of Your Information

We use the information collected for purposes including:

  • Providing and managing our garden design services efficiently.
  • Responding to your inquiries and customer service requests.
  • Sending you marketing communications that may interest you; you may opt out at any time.
  • Improving our website, products, and services.
  • Ensuring website security and preventing unauthorized access.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to recognize you and record your preferences and usage. Cookies help us adapt the site to your needs and enhance your browsing experience.

You may set your browser to refuse cookies or notify you before accepting them. However, some features of the website may not function properly without cookies.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in delivering our services, such as hosting, analytics, and marketing, provided they agree to keep your data confidential.

We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or to protect and defend our rights.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to guard your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no transmission over the internet or electronic storage method is 100% secure.

We regularly review our security protocols to enhance protection.

Your Rights

You have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Accessing the data we hold about you.
  • Requesting correction or deletion.
  • Objecting to or restricting processing.
  • Withdrawing consent where applicable.
  • Data portability in certain situations.

Please contact us using the information below to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites not operated by Leafora Garden Designs. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their policies.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your country. We take precautions to ensure adequate protection consistent with applicable laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to check this page periodically for any updates.
